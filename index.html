Free Roku TV Remote Control — No Ads on Remote Tab

If your physical Roku remote is lost or not working, you can still control your Roku TV using your Android phone. Roku TV Remote Control app helps you reconnect quickly and continue streaming without frustration.

How to Get Started

  1. Download the Roku TV Remote Control app from Google Play Store
  2. Make sure your Android phone and Roku TV are on the same WiFi network
  3. Make sure 'Control by Mobile Apps' is set to 'Permissive' on your Roku TV (Settings › System › Advanced System Settings › Control by Mobile Apps)
  4. Open the app and it will automatically discover your Roku device
  5. Select your device and start controlling your TV
  6. Enjoy seamless Roku remote control from your phone

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use this app without a physical remote?

Yes! That's the main benefit of Roku TV Remote Control. As long as your Roku TV is connected to the same WiFi network, you can control it completely using the app, even if your physical remote is lost or broken.

Does the app support Roku TV volume control?

Yes, supported Roku TVs can control volume directly from the app. You can adjust volume, navigate channels, launch apps, and perform all remote functions through the app.

Is this app free?

Yes, the app is completely free to download and use. We believe everyone should have access to an affordable Roku remote solution.

Which Roku devices are supported?

Roku TV Remote Control works with Roku TV, Roku Streaming Stick, Roku Ultra, Roku Express, and all other Roku devices running Roku OS 5.0 or higher.

Do I need to buy a physical remote if I use this app?

No, you don't need to buy an expensive physical remote. The app provides all the functionality you need to control your Roku device.

Is my connection secure?

Yes, your connection is secure and private. The app connects directly to your Roku device over your local WiFi network. We don't collect any personal data from your Roku TV or send any information to external servers.
