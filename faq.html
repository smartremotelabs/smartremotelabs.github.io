Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Roku TV Remote Control

General Questions

What is Roku TV Remote Control?

Roku TV Remote Control is a free Android app that allows you to control your Roku TV or streaming device directly from your smartphone. It works as a complete replacement for your physical Roku remote, providing all the controls you need without batteries or lost remotes.

Is Roku TV Remote Control free?

Yes. Roku TV Remote Control is free to download and includes essential remote features such as navigation, keyboard input, and quick Roku device connection. Some optional premium features may also be available depending on the app version.

How do I download the app?

You can download Roku TV Remote Control from the Google Play Store on your Android device. Search for "Roku TV Remote Control" or click here to download.

Is the app available for iOS?

Currently, Roku TV Remote Control is available for Android devices only. We're working on bringing the app to iOS in the future. Check back soon or contact us for updates.

Compatibility & Setup

Which Roku devices are supported?

Roku TV Remote Control works with:

  • Roku TV (all models)
  • Roku Streaming Stick
  • Roku Streaming Stick+
  • Roku Ultra
  • Roku Express
  • Roku Premiere
  • Any Roku device running Roku OS 5.0 or higher

What Android version do I need?

Roku TV Remote Control requires Android 5.0 (API level 21) or higher. Most modern Android devices meet this requirement. If your device is very old, you may need to update your Android OS to use the app.

Do my phone and Roku need to be on the same WiFi network?

Yes, both your Android phone and your Roku device must be connected to the same WiFi network for the app to work. The app communicates with your Roku device over your local network, not through the internet.

Can I use the app on multiple devices?

Yes, you can install Roku TV Remote Control on multiple Android devices and use them to control the same Roku TV. This is useful if you have multiple family members with their own phones.

Features & Functionality

Can I use this app without a physical remote?

Yes, absolutely! That's the main purpose of Roku TV Remote Control. You don't need a physical remote at all. The app provides all the functionality of the original Roku remote.

Does the app support all remote functions?

Yes, Roku TV Remote Control supports all major remote functions including:

  • Navigation (Up, Down, Left, Right)
  • OK/Select button
  • Home button
  • Back button
  • Volume control
  • Mute
  • Channel navigation
  • Quick app launch buttons

Can I control volume with the app?

Yes, supported Roku TVs allow volume control through the app. You can adjust volume up/down, mute, and unmute directly from your phone.

Does the app have a keyboard or search function?

Yes, Roku TV Remote Control includes a keyboard feature that allows you to easily type and search on your Roku TV, which is much more convenient than using the remote to enter text.

Can I launch apps from the app?

Yes, you can quickly launch your favorite apps directly from Roku TV Remote Control without navigating through the menu.

Troubleshooting

The app won't connect to my Roku device. What should I do?

Here are some troubleshooting steps:

  1. Make sure your phone and Roku are on the same WiFi network
  2. Restart your Roku device (unplug for 30 seconds, then plug back in)
  3. Restart your phone
  4. Uninstall and reinstall the Roku TV Remote Control app
  5. Check your router/firewall settings - make sure it's not blocking local connections

The app is very slow or laggy. How can I improve performance?

Try these optimization tips:

  • Make sure you have a strong WiFi signal on both devices
  • Close other apps running in the background on your phone
  • Reduce the distance between your phone and WiFi router
  • Restart your router and Roku device
  • Update to the latest version of Roku TV Remote Control

Why does my phone keep disconnecting from my Roku?

This is usually due to WiFi connectivity issues. Try:

  • Moving closer to your WiFi router
  • Restarting your router and Roku device
  • Checking for WiFi interference from other devices
  • Updating your router firmware
If problems persist, contact us for support.

What should I do if a specific button doesn't work?

Some Roku devices may have different capabilities. If a button doesn't work:

  1. Make sure your Roku device supports that function
  2. Try restarting the app and your Roku device
  3. Check that you're using the latest version of the app
  4. Contact us with the device model and button that's not working

Privacy & Security

Is my data secure?

Yes, your data is secure. Roku TV Remote Control only communicates with your Roku device over your local WiFi network. We do not send any personal information to external servers or collect user data.

What data does the app collect?

Roku TV Remote Control collects minimal data:

  • Basic analytics to improve the app (no personal information)
  • Crash reports to identify and fix issues

Is there a password required to use the app?

No, there is no password required. The app connects directly to your Roku device on your local network. You don't need to create an account or log in.
