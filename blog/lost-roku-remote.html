Lost Your Roku Remote? Here's What to Do

Published: May 21, 2026 Reading time: 6 minutes

Losing your Roku remote can be frustrating, but don't worry. We've got several solutions to help you regain control of your Roku TV quickly and easily. Whether you want to find your remote or use an alternative, we'll guide you through your options.

First Steps: Try to Find Your Remote

Before considering replacements, let's try to locate your remote:

  • Check common places: Under the couch cushions, behind furniture, on the nightstand
  • Check with family: Someone else may have moved it or borrowed it
  • Listen for it: Most Roku remotes have a Find Remote feature that makes them beep
  • Search thoroughly: Check your car, office, or other places you frequently visit

Option 1: Use Your Smartphone as a Remote

The fastest and cheapest solution is to use your Android phone to control your Roku TV.

Why This is the Best Option

  • Free - no need to spend $30-50 on a replacement remote
  • Instant - download an app and start controlling immediately
  • Better - easier keyboard input for searching and typing
  • Convenient - your phone is always with you

How to Use Smart Roku Remote

  1. Download Smart Roku Remote from the Google Play Store
  2. Make sure your phone and Roku TV are on the same WiFi network
  3. Open the app and select your Roku device
  4. Start controlling your TV immediately

Option 2: Buy a Replacement Remote

If you want a physical remote, you have several choices:

Official Roku Remote

  • Price: $20-30
  • Availability: Available on Amazon and Roku's website
  • Types: Standard, Voice, or Enhanced remotes
  • Pros: Official product with warranty
  • Cons: Most expensive option

Third-Party Remotes

  • Price: $15-25
  • Availability: Amazon, eBay, Walmart
  • Pros: Often cheaper than official remotes
  • Cons: May not have all features

Option 3: Use Roku Mobile App

Roku offers its own official mobile app for controlling Roku devices:

  • Download the official Roku app from Google Play Store
  • It works similar to third-party apps
  • Provides basic remote control functionality
  • Free to download and use

Preventing Future Loss

Tips to Keep Track of Your Remote

  • Dedicated holder: Keep a remote holder on your table or armrest
  • Label it: Put your phone number on the remote
  • Use a tracker: Attach a Bluetooth tracker (like AirTag) to your remote
  • Backup app: Install Smart Roku Remote as a backup for emergencies
  • Secure placement: Always return the remote to the same spot after use

Comparison: Remote vs. Smartphone App

Physical Remote:

  • Pros: No WiFi needed for basic functions, faster response
  • Cons: Can get lost, batteries die, expensive to replace

Smartphone App (Smart Roku Remote):

  • Pros: Always with you, free, never loses battery, easy typing
  • Cons: Requires WiFi, phone needs to be charged

Recommended Solution

We recommend a hybrid approach:

  1. Install Smart Roku Remote on your phone as a backup
  2. Keep your physical remote in a designated safe place
  3. If you lose your remote, use the app immediately
  4. Use the app regularly to avoid needing to buy a replacement

Troubleshooting: Phone App Not Working?

If you can't get your phone app working:

  • Ensure WiFi is connected on both devices
  • Restart your Roku TV and phone
  • Restart your WiFi router
  • Try moving closer to the router
  • Reinstall the app

Conclusion

Losing your Roku remote doesn't mean you're stuck. With Smart Roku Remote, you can control your Roku TV instantly from your Android phone. It's the fastest, cheapest, and most convenient solution. Download the app today and never worry about a lost remote again!
