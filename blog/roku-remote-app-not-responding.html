    • Roku Remote App Not Responding? Try This Roku TV Setting Fix

    Published: May 26, 2026 Reading time: 5 minutes

    Sometimes a Roku remote app connects to the TV but does not respond correctly.

    You may notice:

    • button presses do nothing
    • the app connects but cannot control the TV
    • volume and navigation stop working
    • the Roku device appears online but ignores commands

    In many cases, the problem is caused by a Roku TV setting related to mobile app access.

    Fortunately, this is usually very easy to fix.

    The Setting That Often Causes the Problem

    Roku TVs include a security setting called:

    "Control by Mobile Apps"

    If this setting is restricted, Roku remote apps may not work properly.

    To allow full communication between your Android phone and Roku TV, the network access mode should typically be set to:

    Permissive

    How to Fix Roku Remote App Not Responding

    Follow these steps directly on your Roku TV.

    Step 1 — Open Roku Settings

    Go to:

    • Settings

    Step 2 — Open System Settings

    Navigate to:

    • System

    Step 3 — Open Advanced System Settings

    Select:

    • Advanced System Settings

    Step 4 — Open Control by Mobile Apps

    Go to:

    • Control by Mobile Apps

    Step 5 — Change Network Access

    Open:

    • Network Access

    Then select:

    • Permissive

    Why This Fix Works

    When Roku network access is restricted:

    • mobile apps may partially connect
    • commands may fail
    • remote control functions may stop responding

    Setting the mode to "Permissive" allows Roku to fully communicate with trusted devices on your local WiFi network.

    This often immediately fixes:

    • unresponsive remote apps
    • failed navigation
    • connection issues
    • delayed controls

    Important Requirements

    Your Phone and Roku Must Be on the Same WiFi Network

    This is one of the most common issues.

    Double-check:

    • phone WiFi
    • Roku WiFi
    • guest network settings

    If they are on different networks, the app may not work.

    Restart Devices After Changing the Setting

    If the app still does not respond after enabling permissive mode:

    Try restarting:

    • your Roku TV
    • your Android phone
    • your WiFi router

    Then reconnect the app.

    Additional Roku Remote Troubleshooting Tips

    If the issue continues, also try:

    • updating Roku software
    • restarting the Roku device
    • reinstalling the remote app
    • reconnecting WiFi
    • disabling VPNs on your phone
    • checking router isolation settings

    Final Thoughts

    If your Roku remote app connects but does not respond correctly, the "Control by Mobile Apps" setting is one of the first things you should check.

    Changing:

    Network Access → Permissive

    often fixes the problem within seconds.
